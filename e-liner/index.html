Activities

Activities for dyslexic learners to practice breaking down words, identifying sounds, and more.

  • Breaking down words into simpler syllables and relating them to usage.
  • Identifying how the same letter can sound differently.
  • Audio exercises to pronounce words syllable-by-syllable and then as a whole.
